Introduction

Routine ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Routine (the "App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Data: Display name, preferences, settings
  • App Content: Routines, habits, notes, progress data
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we don't store credit card details)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Technical Data: IP address, device identifiers, crash logs

Biometric Information

  • Face ID/Touch ID: Used locally on your device for secure note access (never transmitted or stored on our servers)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide App Services: Create and manage your account, sync your data
  • Process Payments: Handle premium subscriptions through Stripe
  • Improve User Experience: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Communication: Send important updates, respond to support requests
  • Security: Protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and technical issues

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Service Providers

We share limited data with trusted service providers:

  • Supabase (Database & Authentication): Stores your account and app data securely
  • Stripe (Payment Processing): Handles premium subscription payments
  • Vercel (Backend Services): Hosts our payment processing infrastructure

We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users' safety, or comply with legal processes.

Data Security

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in secure, encrypted databases
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing monitoring and security assessments

Biometric Security

  • Face ID/Touch ID data never leaves your device
  • Used only for local authentication of secure notes
  • Processed entirely by Apple's Secure Enclave

Your Privacy Rights

Data Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all personal information we have about you
  • Update Information: Modify your profile and preferences anytime
  • Delete Account: Request complete account and data deletion
  • Data Portability: Export your routine and progress data

Communication Preferences

  • Notifications: Control push notifications in app settings
  • Marketing: Opt out of promotional communications anytime

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

Data Retention

Account Data

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backup systems: Completely purged within 90 days

Payment Data

  • Payment records retained for tax and legal compliance (typically 7 years)
  • Credit card information never stored (handled entirely by Stripe)

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information according to this Privacy Policy.
