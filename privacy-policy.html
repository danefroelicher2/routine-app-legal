privacy policy

last updated: january 1, 2025

1. information collection

required permissions:

  • app usage: to monitor and analyze your routine patterns
  • notifications: to send you habit reminders and motivational alerts

2. data usage and protection

your routine information is:

  • analyzed to provide habit insights
  • used to generate progress reports and trends
  • stored locally on your device
  • not shared with third parties
  • not transmitted to our servers

3. data collection and usage

3.1 data not linked to your identity

the following data may be collected but is not linked to your identity:

  • usage data
  • product interaction: how you interact with app features and functionality
  • diagnostics: app performance and crash data

4. data security

we implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

your habit data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. we regularly review our security practices to ensure your information remains protected.

5. your rights

you have the right to:

  • access and update your personal information
  • delete your account and associated data
  • export your habit data
  • opt out of marketing communications

6. contact us

if you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

email: privacy@routineapp.com
